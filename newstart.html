Who are you?
Baby Journey is a web-based application that designed to combine education videos with mini puzzle games and is intended to impart the basic knowledge of human reproduction, gender identity, and body privacy to young children aged from 5 to 10.
Hi, welcome to Baby Journey! In this application, you can explore the knowledge of “Where do Babies Come from”, “Boys and Girls”, and “This is My Body” through watching cute cartoon videos and playing interesting mini games. Now let’s get started!
